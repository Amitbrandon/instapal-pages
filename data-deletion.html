Data Deletion Instructions

At Instapal, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your data from our service.

What Data We Collect

When you use Instapal, we collect and store:

  • Your Instagram username and user ID
  • Profile picture URL
  • Instagram video/post URLs you've saved
  • AI-generated tags and metadata for your bookmarks
  • Message interaction history

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Via Instagram DM (Recommended)

1. Open Instagram and go to @instapal.ai

2. Send a direct message with: "delete my data"

3. You'll receive confirmation within 24 hours that your data has been deleted

Method 2: Via Email

Send an email to instapals.ai@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your Instagram username
  • Confirmation that you want all your data deleted

We will process your request within 30 days and send you confirmation.
