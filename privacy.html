Introduction

Instapal ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Instagram bookmark management service.

Information We Collect

When you use Instapal, we collect:

  • Instagram Profile Information: Username, user ID, profile picture URL
  • Message Content: Instagram URLs you send us and search queries
  • Saved Bookmarks: URLs of Instagram content you want to save
  • AI-Generated Data: Tags, categories, and metadata we create for your bookmarks
  • Usage Data: Interaction timestamps and service usage patterns

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide our bookmark management service
  • Generate AI-powered tags and organization
  • Enable search and retrieval of your saved content
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your data is stored on secure cloud servers and is only accessible to authorized personnel.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes other than providing our service

We may share data only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or property
  • With service providers who help us operate (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your bookmarks
  • Opt-out of our service at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as you use our service. You can request deletion at any time through our Data Deletion page.

Children's Privacy

Instapal is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through Instagram DM or by updating this page.
